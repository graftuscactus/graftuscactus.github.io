Bridgesii KGC x Wowie

This listing is for a 3 month old approximately 3 inches tall Trichocereus Bridgesii (KGC x Wowie) seedling grafted onto Pereskiopsis. Grafted cacti grow much faster than cacti on their own roots and even grow well indoors under artificial lights. The cactus will be shipped bare roots via USPS within 1 business day of receiving the order.
